Deposits for Work Orders

Take a deposit before the wrench turns. Collect payment on any open Work Order, Estimate through Review, and the system auto-applies it the moment you create the invoice. No spreadsheets, no manual reconciliation, and if you're connected to QuickBooks, no double entry either.

What you can do

→  Add Deposit on any open Work Order, no limit per WO
→  Auto-applied to the invoice on creation, balance recalculates
→  Visible on the Work Order Invoice and Customer Payments page
→  Syncs to QuickBooks as unapplied payments, with one-time consent
→  Overpayment? An excess credit is created automatically

02 · NEW
Available

Multiple Bin Locations per Part

No more duplicate Part numbers with location suffixes to fake multi-bin tracking. Assign a single Part to multiple bins with separate quantities per location. 50 brake clean bottles, 20 on the service truck, 30 in the warehouse, one Part record, accurate counts.

What you can do

→  Assign unlimited bin locations per Part with quantity each
→  Pick parts from specific bins on Work Orders and Part Sales
→  Run cycle counts bin by bin with auto-calculated adjustments
→  Auto-route received POs and returns to the right bin
→  Existing inventory migrates automatically to General Storage
Configure Bin Locations
03 · NEW
Rolling out

Custom Roles & Permissions

The default roles, Service Manager, Service Advisor, Foreman, Technician, Parts Manager, Parts Technician, are still there. Now you can clone any of them and tweak the permissions, or build a brand new role from scratch when your shop has a job description we didn't anticipate.

What you can do

→  Clone any default role as a starting point, then customize
→  Toggle permissions individually or by group: Work Orders, Scheduling, Parts, Customers
→  See how many users are assigned to each role at a glance
→  Permission changes apply in real time to everyone in the role
→  Owner, Administrator, Office and Time Clock User stay protected
